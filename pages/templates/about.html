About Us

“We aim to build the bridge between customers and businesses.”

Who we are?

AroundTz is the platform which takes your business to the face of customers, through free business advertisements.

We see a strong need to foster local businesses in an advanced convention and a need to connect with customers for easy shopping.

We are not based on only promoting businesses with precedence over meeting customers needs. We offer equal opportunities to both business owners to advertise and customers to shop with us.

Our Mission

“We aim to increase your daily sales and meeting peoples needs by connecting your business (es) with customers around your business location.”

Our Values

Customer

Business Owners

AroundTz is a special site beneficially for anyone with his/her business (es). Once you have a business (es) you will always want it to be widely known but also putting enough efforts to make it grow big each day. As advertisements nowadays are too expensive to afford by most people who own business (es) in Tanzania, AroundTz offers you a free field to advertise all your businesses and we promote all you have to increase your daily sales by connecting you with numerous numbers of customers who will be using our site to shop.
We carry the motive of taking your business (es) to the next levels as among of our business goals and we believe all together we can achieve it better.

Customers Safety

Safety for Buyers

● Always see and inspect carefuly the product before buying.

● Meet the seller at his/her office.

● Ask how the product will be delivered.

● Agree how payment and delivery will be made.

● Don’t send cash until you know you want to buy the product.

Safety for Seller

● Ensure to upload an accurate and clear image of the product.

● Ensure that your registered contacts are always active.

● Ensure you direct and meet the buyers at your office.

● Be aware of fake messages, demand a phone number.
