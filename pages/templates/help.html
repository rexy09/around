How to use AroundTz

The following are some of things you need to do and know when you want to join us: (i) To sign into our site, create a business account (you will be demanded to fill some of your vital details in order to sign in successfully). Note: Make sure all your details are true and that you can easily be reached via your contacts (phone number & Email).

(ii) It is important to you to correctly share your location for customers to get access with you when they are searching for nearby services around from which the results will be displayed on the map.

(iii) We offer you with calls and chats feature where you can use it to make direct calls with your customers and chat with them via SMS.

(iv) We also have a ground for you to post your products and advertise your business (es) on your own page for FREE. You can use this feature anytime and it will only work with you when you have enough data.
